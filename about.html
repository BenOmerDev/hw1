Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) is becoming commonplace in business and engineering pursuits. Understanding the basic principles behind AI will enable us to better interact with and utilize this technology.

Tic Tac Toe AI improves its ability to play the game through reinforcement learning. Winning a game results in a positive reward. A "neural network" of code assesses the moves made in each game, and adjusts itself in order to win games and mazimize rewards.

By participating in the training of Tic Tac Toe AI, you gain the following:

  • Deeper understanding of how AI works
  • Name posted on the Leaderboard (for top 10 participants)
  • Merchandise raffle entry for each 10th game completed
